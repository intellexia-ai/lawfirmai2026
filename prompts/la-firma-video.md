# La Firma — Video Prompt

## Scene Concept
Modern law firm building at night with starry sky. Professional, international, premium aesthetic.

## Visual Elements
- **Primary Subject**: Modern office building with glass and steel architecture
- **Time of Day**: Night with clear starry sky visible
- **Sky**: Deep dark blue transitioning to black with visible stars and constellations
- **Building Details**: 
  - Illuminated windows showing activity inside
  - Clean lines, contemporary architecture
  - Subtle warm lighting on facade
  - Professional corporate environment

## Camera Movement
- Slow pan upward from street level to rooftop
- Building reveals gradually against starry backdrop
- Smooth, cinematic movement
- Final frame shows full building against starfield

## Lighting Specification
- **Ambient**: Night, clear sky with stars
- **Building Lights**: Warm white (3000K) from interior windows
- **Exterior Lighting**: Subtle upward accent lighting on building
- **Sky**: Deep indigo to black with visible stars and Milky Way

## Mood & Style
- Professional, aspirational, international
- Clean, modern, minimalist
- Premium corporate aesthetic
- Calm, confident, stable

## Technical Specs
- **Duration**: 10-15 seconds
- **Format**: MP4, 1280x720, 30fps
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Color Grading**: Cool tones, high contrast, professional grade
